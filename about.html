About

A considered archive for hand-pulled monotype prints.

brpartsandcraftscoza presents a focused body of signed, unframed monotype works. The site is built to make each print easy to view, compare, and inquire about without losing the calm of a gallery visit.

Process

Surface, pressure, paper, and the single moment of transfer.

Monotype printing keeps a direct record of gesture and pressure. Every work carries small variations in tone and edge that make the image singular rather than editioned.

Collecting

Clear artwork pages for confident decisions.

Each listing includes availability, pricing, scale, descriptive notes, and inquiry links so collectors and curators can move from browsing to conversation quickly.
